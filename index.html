Mighty Morphin Power Rangers - Season 1: Five teenagers are given the ability to morph into "Power Rangers" by Zordon. They're tasked with stoping Rita Repulsa's evil schemes.

Mighty Morphin Power Rangers - Season 2: A new villain, Lord Zedd, seals Rita away and takes over as the main villain for the Rangers. With the help of Tommy's new White Ranger powers, the new crew must work together to take down Lord Zedd.

Mighty Morphin Power Rangers - Season 3: Aided by her brother, Rito, Rita and Zedd manage to destroy the Rangers' ThunderZords. The Rangers are force to search for Ninjor, the original creator of the Power Coins, in order to obtain the mighty Ninja Powers.

Big Bad Beetleborgs - Season 1: Three "typical average kids" enter the supposedly haunted Hillhurst Mansion where they free the mysterious Flabber from an old organ pipe. Upon releasing him, he grant them the wish to become their favorite comic book heroes, the Big Bad Beetleborgs.

Big Bad Beetleborgs - Season 2: The Crustaceans have ousted the Magnavores as the Beetleborgs' new arch-enemies. Nukus discovers that his creator is actually Art's incarcerated brother, Les Fortunes. Now, Art must create new powers, armor, and weapons for the Beetelborgs in order for them to combat his brother's creations.
